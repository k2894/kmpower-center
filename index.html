About Us

At K-Mpower Centre, we believe every survivor deserves access to support, healing and hope. Our mission is to provide affordable recovery resources and emotional support services for individuals affected by gender-based violence.

Through therapy sessions, support groups, wellness workshops and medical hampers, we aim to empower survivors to rebuild their confidence and improve their well-being.
